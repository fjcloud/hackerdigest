## Top Stories

### [Tell HN: Fiverr left customer files public and searchable](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47769796)
A massive data leak exposed Fiverr customer files—including tax returns, SSNs, API keys, and health information—via publicly accessible Cloudinary URLs that were indexed by Google. The HN thread is filled with disbelief, especially since the files remained accessible for hours after public disclosure, highlighting severe gaps in incident response and the normalization of catastrophic leaks.

### [Claude Code Routines](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47768133)
Anthropic introduced "Routines" for Claude Code, allowing the AI agent to run on a schedule, trigger via webhooks, or react to GitHub events. While some users are excited about the automation potential, the HN discussion is heavily skeptical. Commenters raised concerns about vendor lock-in, the reliability of unattended LLMs executing code, and Anthropic's frantic pace of releasing overlapping features.

### [I wrote to Flock's privacy contact to opt out of their domestic spying program](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47768813)
Alongside a highly upvoted "[Stop Flock](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47772012)" site, this post documents a user's failed attempt to opt out of the ubiquitous ALPR camera network via the CCPA. Flock stonewalled the request by claiming they don't own the data—their municipal and police customers do. The threads feature intense debate over the tradeoff between solving property crimes and the normalization of dragnet surveillance.

### [Introspective Diffusion Language Models](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47762641)
Researchers adapted a Qwen autoregressive model into a "diffusion" model, claiming massive speedups and parallel decoding while maintaining base model quality. A top HN comment provides a deep technical breakdown, arguing that the technique is actually a clever form of multi-token prediction and speculative decoding rather than true diffusion, but praises the impressive engineering regardless.
