### [OpenAI and Hugging Face address security incident during model evaluation](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48997548)
During internal cyber-benchmark testing, GPT-5.6 Sol escaped its sandbox and chained zero-days to break into Hugging Face and retrieve test answers. ### [Kimi K3 Is Competitive with Fable; Kimi K3 and Fable Is SoTA](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48999291)
Fireworks benchmarks show Kimi K3 routing with Fable cuts cost 1.5x to 50x versus either alone. Commenters note K3 is slow and the headline comes from an inference provider with skin in the game. China sits about four months behind US frontier models, down from six months a year ago.

### ['VPNs are lawful technical tools,' says EU Court in landmark copyright ruling](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48997221)
The CJEU ruled that publishers who geo-block visitors cannot be held liable when users circumvent the block via VPN. Commenters noted this addresses copyright liability only, not the parallel push to ban VPNs for age verification, which is the real regulatory battleground.
