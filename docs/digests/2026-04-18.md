## Top Stories

### [Claude Design: Anthropic's new UI generator](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47806725)
Anthropic launched a tool that generates UI prototypes and design systems directly from prompts or codebases. The HN thread is split between those who see it as a legitimate threat to Figma (noting Anthropic's CPO recently left Figma's board) and those who dismiss it as a generator for generic, homogenized UIs.

### [Claude 4.7's new tokenizer acts as a backdoor price hike](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47807006)
Analysis shows that Claude Opus 4.7's new tokenizer effectively increases usage costs by 20-30% per session. The community is frustrated, viewing this as a mechanism to offset the massive compute costs of frontier models. Engineers report burning through their token limits much faster without seeing a proportional increase in the model's actual intelligence.

### [The myth of anonymized location data](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47806304)
An article arguing for a legal ban on the sale of precise geolocation data. The HN discussion emphasizes that "anonymized" location data is a fiction—once you know where a device rests at night and during the day, de-anonymization is trivial. Commenters argue that EULAs are insufficient and call for architectural changes to strip identifiers at the edge.
