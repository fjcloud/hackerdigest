## Top Stories

### [Andrej Karpathy joins Anthropic](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48194352)
Andrej Karpathy announced he is joining Anthropic to work on pre-training, effectively pausing his education startup Eureka Labs. The HN discussion noted this as a major talent win for Anthropic and debated whether it signals that the frontier of AI research is now too capital-intensive for independent labs to compete.

### [Google releases Gemini 3.5 Flash with a massive price hike](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48196570)
Google launched Gemini 3.5 Flash, but the community reaction was overwhelmingly negative due to the pricing. At $1.50 per million input tokens and $9.00 for output, it costs roughly 3x more than previous Flash models. Commenters noted that despite strong benchmarks, the model's real-world coding and tool-use capabilities still lag behind Anthropic and OpenAI.

### [GCP suspends Railway's account](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48201484)
Cloud platform Railway experienced a major outage after Google Cloud abruptly blocked their account. The incident reignited the perennial HN debate about GCP's notoriously automated and opaque support systems. While Railway managed to restore service, commenters warned against building startups with a single point of failure on Google's infrastructure.

### [GitHub investigates unauthorized access to internal repos](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48201316)
GitHub announced via X that they are investigating unauthorized access to their internal repositories, with attackers claiming to have exfiltrated ~3,800 repos. While customer data appears unaffected, the breach sparked discussions about the increasing frequency of supply chain attacks and whether the proliferation of AI coding agents is accelerating vulnerability discovery.
