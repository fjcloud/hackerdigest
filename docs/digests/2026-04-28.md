## Top Stories

### [Microsoft and OpenAI end their exclusive and revenue-sharing deal](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47921248)
Microsoft and OpenAI have ended their exclusive partnership, allowing OpenAI to use other cloud providers like AWS or Google Cloud. The HN discussion notes that the previous agreement was likely kneecapping OpenAI's ability to scale, and this move opens the door for OpenAI to utilize Google's TPUs instead of relying solely on Azure.

### [4TB of voice samples just stolen from 40k AI contractors at Mercor](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47919630)
A massive data breach at AI contractor Mercor leaked 4TB of voice samples paired with ID document scans. Commenters point out that this specific combination is particularly dangerous, essentially providing attackers with a "deepfake-ready kit" that can be used for banking voiceprint bypass and identity fraud.

### [Networking changes coming in macOS 27](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47923010)
Apple is dropping support for the Apple Filing Protocol (AFP) in macOS 27, forcing users to switch to SMB. The thread highlights that this permanently breaks native Time Machine backups for older Apple Time Capsules, though some engineers are already compiling Samba 4 to keep their legacy hardware alive.
